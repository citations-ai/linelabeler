3:37:1764:41:29:71:538:630:144:g_d0_f4:144:g_d0_f4:The term ìcycleî is used to describe a process that moves sequentially between a series of
3:38:1854:29:29:71:538:601:144:g_d0_f4:144:g_d0_f4:clearly identifiable phases in a recurrent or periodic fashion.  Economists of the nineteenth
3:39:1947:29:29:71:538:572:144:g_d0_f4:144:g_d0_f4:and early twentieth centuries were persuaded that they saw such a pattern exhibited in the

3:42:2221:29:28:71:538:485:144:g_d0_f4:144:g_d0_f4:enduring summaries of what seems to happen during the respective phases were provided
3:43:2306:28:29:71:361:457:144:g_d0_f4:144:g_d0_f4:by Mitchell (1927, 1951) and Burns and Mitchell (1946).
3:44:2361:29:29:89:539:428:144:g_d0_f4:144:g_d0_f4:The expression ìbusiness cycle theoryî remains in common usage today, even though,
3:45:2443:29:29:71:538:399:144:g_d0_f4:144:g_d0_f4:in most of the modern models that wear the label, there in fact is no business cycle in the
3:46:2534:29:29:71:539:370:144:g_d0_f4:144:g_d0_f4:sense just described.  These are models of economicfluctuations, to be sure, but they do
3:47:2622:29:29:71:538:341:144:g_d0_f4:144:g_d0_f4:not exhibit clearly articulated phases through which the economy could be said to pass in a
3:48:2713:29:29:71:162:312:144:g_d0_f4:144:g_d0_f4:recurrent pattern.
3:49:2731:29:29:89:539:283:144:g_d0_f4:144:g_d0_f4:In part this shift in the professionís conception of what needs to be explained about
3:50:2816:29:29:71:539:254:144:g_d0_f4:144:g_d0_f4:businessfluctuations reflects a desire to integrate the determinants of long-run economic
3:51:2905:29:28:71:539:225:144:g_d0_f4:144:g_d0_f4:growth and the causes of short-run economic downturns within a single unified theory of
3:52:2992:28:29:71:538:197:144:g_d0_f4:144:g_d0_f4:aggregate economic performance.  Since improvements in overall productivity are widely

4:57:3445:0:29:71:539:671:144:g_d0_f4:144:g_d0_f4:(1972) were thefirst to incorporate stochastic variation in the rate of technical progress

20:519:30543:40:29:71:538:417:144:g_d0_f4:144:g_d0_f4:We now return to the question posed in the title of this paper, ìWhatís real about the

20:528:31316:28:29:71:539:157:144:g_d0_f4:144:g_d0_f4:linear specification.  Each of these series seems to exhibit different dynamic behavior in
20:529:31406:29:29:71:538:128:144:g_d0_f4:144:g_d0_f4:recessions and expansions.  The unemployment rate rises more quickly than it falls, and
20:530:31493:29:0:71:538:99:144:g_d0_f4:144:g_d0_f4:this often occurs at the same time as rapid spikes up and then back down in short-term
