39:913:74249:27:28:126:539:380:144:g_d0_f4:144:g_d0_f4:The consequences and desirability of capital account liberalization among developing 
39:914:74334:28:27:90:532:352:144:g_d0_f4:144:g_d0_f4:countries is likely to remain a topic of debate for the foreseeable future.  People on one side

39:920:74858:27:28:126:508:187:144:g_d0_f4:144:g_d0_f4:In this paper we have surveyed what current research is able to tell us about the 
39:921:74940:28:27:90:534:159:144:g_d0_f4:144:g_d0_f4:consequences of capital account liberalization.  We point out that, while industrial countries

39:923:75125:28:28:90:532:104:144:g_d0_f4:144:g_d0_f4:more widespread capital account liberalization among developing countries, the majority of 
39:924:75216:28:0:90:528:76:144:g_d0_f4:144:g_d0_f4:developing countries retain controls over capital flows.  The evidence on the effects of this 
40:925:75348:0:28:90:538:684:144:g_d0_f4:144:g_d0_f4:are somewhat mixed.  We have shown that empirical evidence presented in existing research 
40:926:75438:28:28:90:510:656:144:g_d0_f4:144:g_d0_f4:does not strongly point towards a general result concerning the consequences of capital 
40:927:75526:28:27:90:533:628:144:g_d0_f4:144:g_d0_f4:account liberalization.  There is mixed evidence that capital account liberalization promotes 
40:928:75620:27:28:90:524:601:144:g_d0_f4:144:g_d0_f4:long-run economic growth.  Our own regression results suggest that these effects are most

40:930:75800:27:28:90:497:546:144:g_d0_f4:144:g_d0_f4:suggests a more pronounced effect of capital account liberalization among industrial 
40:931:75885:28:28:90:445:518:144:g_d0_f4:144:g_d0_f4:countries than among a more broadly defined set of developing countries. 
40:932:75958:28:27:126:538:490:144:g_d0_f4:144:g_d0_f4:Given the importance of this topic, the lack of a clear consensus in the literature, and,
