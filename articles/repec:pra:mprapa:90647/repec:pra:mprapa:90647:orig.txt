27:716:37701:25:26:56:541:614:8:g_d0_f10:8:g_d0_f10:groups of countries (Mariani et al., 2012). On top of a substantial regulation established through multilateral

27:718:37919:25:25:56:541:563:8:g_d0_f10:8:g_d0_f10:of trade agreements: their impact on trade is not always clear, nor quantified at global scale. We assessed the 
27:719:38031:25:25:56:541:538:8:g_d0_f10:8:g_d0_f10:effects  of  country-specific  NTMs  on  global  imports  of  wine,  through  a  gravity  model  approach.  We

27:722:38361:25:25:56:331:462:8:g_d0_f10:8:g_d0_f10:segments of the wine market (sparkling, bottled, bulk, musts). 
27:723:38424:25:26:56:541:437:8:g_d0_f10:8:g_d0_f10:We found that country-specific NTMs favour trade, by increasing imports of wine. Moreover, we show that 
27:724:38528:26:25:56:541:411:8:g_d0_f10:8:g_d0_f10:the effects of the SPSs are similar (and large) for all types of market segment (sparkling, bottled, and bulk).
